This is an archive of film production design that contributes to a strong sense of world-building. Generally, the production design featured in this archive creates a very specific version of reality within the world of the film. This reality tends to be different from our world and unique to the film itself. While this may sound like many fantasy and sci-fi films (and you will definitely find those here), the archive is not limited by genre and instead includes any film with a distinctive and transportive visual style.

This archive aims to:

(a) showcase the work of production designers, their teams, and other artists working in film;

(b) provide inspiration to other filmmakers and artists;

(c) disseminate information about production design in films.
